Who We Are

Kappa Theta Phi II — the Wokou-Corsairs — is a brotherhood forged in the fires of loyalty, discipline, and unbreakable bonds. Rooted in the spirit of the samurai and the relentless will of the corsair, we stand as one unit, one family.

Our fraternity operates through a structured system of Fractions, each led by a Daimyo and Shogun, ensuring every brother has purpose, direction, and a path to greatness. From initiation to legacy, we build men of substance.

United under the Iron Fleet — our founding compass — we navigate the waters of Second Life with honor, strength, and an unshakeable commitment to each other.
